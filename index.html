About Us

Established since 2014, Robotics Club of SMJK Hua Lian, Taiping is now a growing club in the school. Although not much members are in this club now, we're still strong. For the past few years, we have successfully trained a few newbies from zero to hero and won several prizes in state level competitions like National Robotics Competition (NRC) - Perak.

In 2015, 3 teams were sent to participate in Regular Category in NRC - Perak. Both the 3 teams won First Place as well as Second and Third Places. For Open Category, 1 team was sent and we won the First Runner-up, Best Presentation Award and Best Teamwork Award.

Our gold is to enhance STEM skills of our members, at the same time boost their creativity to solve daily life problems with the use of robotics. For now, we collect RM38 per month from our members. All the fees will be sent to our trainers from RoboTop Educationalist, a famous robotics training center in Taiping, Perak.

We are currently using fully air-conditioned Computer Lab in SMJK Hua Lian, Taiping for our training. Our training starts from 7:30 a.m. until 9:30 a.m. every Saturday. Lego Mindstorms EV3s are our main teaching materials.
